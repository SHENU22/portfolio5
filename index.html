Our Sanctuary

A Legacy of Spiritual Guidance

Founded on the principles of the Buddha's teachings, Weluwanarama has served as a beacon of light for the local and international community for over four decades. Nestled in a tranquil setting, the center offers a refuge from the chaos of modern life.

Under the guidance of our Chief Incumbent, Venerable Dr. Bodhi Thero, we strive to preserve the authenticity of the Theravada tradition while making it accessible to the modern seeker.
